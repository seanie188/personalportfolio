What is PLMUN?

The Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa is a local-government-funded university in Muntinlupa City, Philippines. Established in 1991—originally as Muntinlupa Polytechnic College—it has grown to a premier institution dedicated to providing quality tertiary education to the "Muntinlupeños".

The university is known for its commitment to academic excellence and social responsibility, offering various programs including Information Technology, Education, and Criminal Justice. Located within the scenic NBP Reservation in Poblacion, PLMun continues to be a hub for student development, leadership, and community service.
